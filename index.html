About me

I am a B.Tech Junior Undergraduate at IIT Gandhinagar, pursuing Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Management. I am passionate about Data Analysis, Graphic design, Web design, Entrepreneurship, Leadership and Management. I enjoy meeting new people and hearing new perspectives, and I am always enthusiastic about trying out new initiatives and working in unconventional ways.

Throughout my academic journey, I have actively taken up numerous positions of responsibility to contribute to the college community. These roles include serving as a Class Representative, Coordinator for Event Management and Design Team at Amalthea (IIT Gandhinagar's annual tech summit), Secretary of Vinteo (the Filmmaking club of IIT GN), Student Representative at the Media and Communications committee (IIT Gandhinagar) and Coordinator for Class Representatives and Course Feedback.

After my 10th school, I started my first educational startup (Quarksolutions). I designed and implemented an online platform to deliver mock tests, enabling students to practice and assess their knowledge and skills in a simulated exam environment.
